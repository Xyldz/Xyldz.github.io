Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026 · Contact: sethunter.app@gmail.com

Overview

SetHunter ("the app", "we") is a trading-card collection tracker. We are committed to your privacy. The app is designed to keep your collection data on your own device. This policy explains what little data is involved and how the third-party services we use handle it.

Data you create stays on your device

Your collection, wishlist, custom sets, binders, settings and progress are stored locally on your device. We do not have a user account system and we do not upload or store this data on our servers. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it. You can export a CSV backup yourself from within the app.

Card & price data

The app fetches card details, images and market prices from third-party data providers: pokemontcg.io, TCGdex (Japanese/Korean cards) and PokéWallet (price history). These requests include only the card or set you are viewing and your search terms, plus standard technical data your device sends to any website (such as your IP address). They do not include any account or collection data — we have no account system.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the app shows ads served by Google AdMob. To deliver ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain data, which can include:

  • a device advertising identifier;
  • IP address and approximate (non-precise) location;
  • device and app information and ad-interaction data.

This data is used to serve and measure ads and to limit how often you see the same ad. On iOS, we ask for your permission (App Tracking Transparency) before any tracking for personalised ads; you can decline and still use the app. In the UK, EEA and Switzerland, we show Google's consent (UMP) form before serving personalised ads, and you can change your choice at any time from Settings → Ad privacy choices in the app. You can learn more and manage choices here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data / advertising

App updates

The app checks for over-the-air updates from Expo's update service. This sends basic device and version information (platform, app/runtime version) so the correct update can be returned. See the Expo Privacy Policy.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your rights

Because we don't hold your collection data on a server, the simplest way to erase the data the app stores is to delete the app. For data processed by Google for advertising, you can exercise choices through your device's ad settings and the Google links above. If you are in the UK/EU, you have rights under UK GDPR / GDPR; contact us at the email at the top of this page with any request or question.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, when cloud sync and accounts arrive in a future version). The "last updated" date above will change when we do.
